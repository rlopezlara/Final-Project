Our Story

Blockbuster Video is an American best video streaming platform. It was founded by David Cook in 1985 as a stand-alone mom-and-pop home video streaming service, but later grew into a national store chain featuring video game rentals, DVD-by-mail, streaming, video on demand, and cinema theater. The company expanded internationally throughout the 1990s. At its peak in 2004, Blockbuster consisted of 9,094 stores and employed approximately 84,300 people: 58,500 in the United States and 25,800 in other countries.
